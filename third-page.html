Multimedia work

Softball recruitment

Kaylee Schreiner · Softball recruiting faces changes amidst COVID-19 pandemic

This multimedia piece details in impact of COVID-19 on softball players trying to get recruited to play in college. The entire piece was done by me in April of 2020. I conducted all interviews, gathered sounds and wrote and produced the text and audio pieces.

Full text of the story can be found at kayleeschreiner.com

Missouri river flooding

Kaylee Schreiner · Historic floods drown small town business in mid-Missouri

This multimedia package details the impact the flooding of the Missouri River has on small towns and businesses along it. The piece was produced by myself and three other journalists in February of 2020. During the reporting process, I set up and conducted interviews, gathered natural sound and wrote the text piece.

Full text of the story can be found at kayleeschreiner.com

Hermann wine industry

Missouri Business Alert · Speaking Startup: The pandemic's toll on a tourism town

This project was the second part of a Missouri Business Alert series about industries that dominate Missouri towns. Myself and three other reporters focused on the wine industry in Hermann, Missouri and created a package with audio, text, photo, and social media elements.

In this installment, I conducted and set up interviews, as well as wrote the text piece. Full text of the story can be found at kayleeschreiner.com or missouribusinessalert.com

Missouri elections

Kaylee Schreiner · In Mid-Missouri, Marginalized Voices are Pushing for Change
Kaylee Schreiner · A Look at Modern Voter Suppression in Missouri
Kaylee Schreiner · After Amendment 3: It Passed in 2020, but the Coalitions That Fought it Remain Strong

This project is a three-part series that myself and three other journalists produced for KBIA. The first installment details community organizers advocating for change before the election, the second focuses on voter suppression tactics in the state of Missouri during the 2020 election, and the third follows the impact of Amendment 3 after it passed.

During the reporting process, I set up and conducted interviews, gathered natural sound and wrote the first and second text piece, as well as created an infographic and social content.

The stories on the left are in order of when they were released from first to third, and tell a complete story that way. The text pieces that accompany them can be found at kayleeschreiner.com or kbia.org
